About the Project
==================================

**Ploggify** is a service designed to combine exercise and environmental protection by gamifying "plogging",  an activity of picking up litter while jogging.

The name "Ploggify" is a compound word of **Plogging** and **Gamify**, clearly conveying our goal of making plogging a fun and engaging activity.

Mission Statement
---------------------------

Our core objective is to motivate users regarding exercise and environmental protection by enabling them to easily record their plogging activities and share them with others.

Key Features
---------------------------

* **Route Recommendation:** Recommends optimal running routes based on the user's purpose, goals, and region.
* **AI Object Detection:** Uses AI to categorize and count the number of items when the user uploads a final photo of the collected trash.
* **Personal Dashboard:** Check statistics such as total running distance and the amount of trash collected.
* **SNS:** A community feature to share plogging achievements and compete with others.

Main Technology Stack
---------------------------------------

* **Frontend:** Flutter (Dart)
* **Backend:** JAVA 21 (Spring Boot)
* **AI/ML:** Python, YOLO (Roboflow)
* **Interface:** REST API (JSON)
